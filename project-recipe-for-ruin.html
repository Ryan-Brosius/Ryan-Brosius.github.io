What is Recipe for Ruin?

Recipe for Ruin is a game where Enter the Gungeon meets food! Explore the recipe ruins by defeating monsters using a dangerous combination of foods!

Together with a group of 18 others we worked over the course of 20 weeks to create this game in unity. I was one of the lead programmers for this project where I worked on many of the game systems including the enemy director, enemy AI, and my favorite the procedural generation! I also was the lead perforce admin and worked to review integration changes and set up stream information.
