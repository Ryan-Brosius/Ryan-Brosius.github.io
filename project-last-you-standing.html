LAST YOU STANDING?

LAST YOU STANDING is a action arcade-y like game made for the 2025 GMTK game jam. The theme of this jam was "Loop". Through this theme, our team thought of a roman empire that held time-bending colosseum fights where you fight the past versions of yourself!

Together with two other group memebers we worked on this game jam for a total of 3 days in unity. I was the lead programmer for this project where I designed pretty much all of the non UI systems of the game. A few of the important systems were the global leader board, the character controller, the audio management, and my favorite the loop system that allows objects to re-do their previous actions. I had a lot of fun figuring out how to mimick player movements during subsequent loops of the game.
