Coin Laundromat?

Coin Laundromat is an arcade-like rouge-like like (I know too many likes) game made for Brackeys Game Jam 2025.2. The theme of this jam was "Risk it for the biscuit". For this theme our team thought of flipping coins in order to reach a sort of goal that you need to get. We got a little carried away and we have fun gimmicks that the coins have to affect your score when you flip them.

Together with two other group memebers we worked on this game jam for a total of 7~ days in unity. I was the lead programmer for this project where I designed a lot of the core systems of the game. A few of these systems was the coin flipping / general gameplay loop of the game, the purchasing section of the game where you can buy new coins, the dialog system, and many other tiny systems.
I also did the sound design for this game! I have experience doing sound in the past for smaller projects, but this was my first time as the main design lead for a project. It was a lot of fun to use audacity to bit crunch sounds to fit with the visual theming of our game.
